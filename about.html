About Us

BSDS Community at ISI Bangalore

The Bachelor of Statistical Data Science (BSDS) program at ISI Bangalore is a vibrant, student-driven community passionate about statistics, data science, and mathematics. We foster a culture of curiosity, collaboration, and innovation, empowering students to explore, create, and lead in the world of data.

Our community organizes projects, research, talks, and events, and is home to math enthusiasts who love to learn and share knowledge. Whether you're interested in machine learning, pure mathematics, or real-world data applications, you'll find a place here.
