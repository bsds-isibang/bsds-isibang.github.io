About Us

Where Statistics Meets Innovation

The BSDS program at ISI Bangalore is a rigorous undergraduate course designed to cultivate the next generation of data scientists and statisticians. Our student community drives innovation through collaborative projects, research initiatives, and knowledge sharing.

We believe in learning by doing—combining theoretical foundations with practical applications to solve real-world problems using statistical methods and modern data science techniques.

Rigorous Curriculum

Deep foundations in probability, statistics, and computational methods
